FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free plan? +

Yes! Our free plan includes 3 projects with access to basic components. No credit card required.

What frameworks does code export support? +

We support React, Vue, Svelte, and plain HTML. Styling options include Tailwind CSS, CSS Modules, and Styled Components.

Can I use my own design system? +

Absolutely. Import your design tokens, fonts, and color palette. Synth will use them across all AI-generated designs.

How does the AI canvas work? +

Describe your layout in plain language. Our AI interprets your intent, generates a responsive design, and renders it live on the canvas.
